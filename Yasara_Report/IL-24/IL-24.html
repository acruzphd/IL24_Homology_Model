The overall Z-scores for all models have been calculated as the weighted averages of the individual Z-scores using the formula Overall = 0.145*Dihedrals + 0.390*Packing1D + 0.465*Packing3D. The overall score thus captures the correctness of backbone- (Ramachandran plot) and side-chain dihedrals, as well as packing interactions. It applies to globular proteins only, and can be mislead by artificial structures like long single alpha helices (which have perfect dihedrals and are free of packing errors, since there is no packing).
